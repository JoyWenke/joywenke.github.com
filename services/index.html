Audience Configuration

Joy works with any combination of educators, to meet the identified goals of a school or district. Based on these goals, she can present to whole staffs, grade level groups, individual teachers, or a combination of audience configurations.

Strategic Planning

Working with leadership teams and administrators, Joy facilitates the identification of instructional and curricular targets. This results in a professional development plan that meets the current needs of both teachers and students.

Timing Options

Each school or district is unique in specific needs and concerns. There are a number of options available when considering the professional development when Joy works with educators. Due to the unfortunate trend in education to bring in too many initiatives, and different ones each year, teachers have the mentality of “this too shall pass”. Successful staff development is thoughtful, on-going, and consistent. (Transforming Professional Development into Student Results Reeves 2010,) An assessment the teacher needs and expertise determines the amount and duration of in-services or coaching.

Possible Options Include:

  • All staff training (one-time basis)
  • Facilitative training and coaching throughout the year to develop long-term success and efficacy
